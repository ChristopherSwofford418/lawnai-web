Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 8, 2026

LawnAI ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the LawnAI mobile application for AI-powered lawn and yard diagnosis.

Information We Collect

  • Account information: Email address and password when you register
  • Lawn photos: Photos you submit for AI diagnosis (not permanently stored after analysis)
  • Diagnosis history: Past diagnoses, conditions identified, and treatment notes
  • Lawn profile: Grass type, yard size, region, and problem type you provide
  • Usage data: Screens visited and features used within the app
  • Device information: Device type, operating system, and app version

Photo Analysis and AI Consent

Photos you submit for lawn diagnosis are sent to our AI system for analysis and are not permanently stored on our servers after analysis is complete. Before any photo is processed, you will be explicitly asked for your consent. You may decline AI analysis at any time.

How We Use Your Information

  • To diagnose lawn problems and generate treatment recommendations using AI
  • To provide, maintain, and improve the LawnAI service
  • To process your subscription through Apple App Store or Google Play
  • To send important service notifications
  • To respond to your support requests

Data Storage and Security

Your account and diagnosis history are stored securely using Supabase, a SOC 2 compliant cloud database provider. We use industry-standard encryption for all data in transit and at rest. Your data is retained for as long as your account is active.

Subscriptions and Payments

LawnAI offers a monthly subscription at $4.99/month. All payments are processed through Apple App Store or Google Play. We do not store your payment card information. Subscriptions auto-renew unless canceled at least 24 hours before the end of the billing period.

Third-Party Services

  • Supabase — secure data storage and authentication
  • OpenAI — AI photo analysis and lawn diagnosis
  • Apple / Google — payment processing and app distribution

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes. Your data is shared only with the service providers listed above as necessary to operate the app.

Your Rights

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data by contacting us
  • Correction: Update your information through account settings
  • Deletion: Delete your account and all data via Settings > Delete Account
  • Opt-out: Decline AI photo analysis at any time

Children's Privacy

LawnAI is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via the app or email. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
